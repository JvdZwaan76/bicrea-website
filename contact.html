Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our services and process

What types of properties do you specialize in?

We specialize in luxury residential properties, commercial real estate, and mineral rights across the United States. Our portfolio includes coastal estates, urban developments, historic properties, and strategic mineral acquisitions.

How do you ensure the accuracy of mineral title reports?

Our team conducts exhaustive research through county records, state databases, and federal archives. We verify ownership chains, check for liens and encumbrances, and ensure full regulatory compliance with both state and federal standards.

What is the minimum investment threshold?

Our investment opportunities typically start at $1 million. However, we also provide consultation and title services for smaller transactions. We believe in building long-term relationships regardless of initial investment size.

How long does the investment process take?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the transaction. Typically, our due diligence process takes 30-60 days, while complete transactions can range from 60-120 days. We prioritize thoroughness over speed.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with international investors. We can assist with structuring investments to comply with US regulations and provide guidance on tax implications and legal requirements.

What sets Bicrea apart from other firms?

Our 30+ years of experience, meticulous attention to detail, and commitment to client success distinguish us. We combine deep market knowledge with cutting-edge analysis tools and maintain the highest standards of integrity in every transaction.
