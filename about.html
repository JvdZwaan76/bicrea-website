Our Journey

Bicrea was born from a vision to transcend the ordinary in property investments and mineral title services. Established in Florida, a land of opportunity and natural wealth, we have grown into a national authority, serving clients with an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our journey began over 30 years ago, when our founders recognized a gap in the market for sophisticated, client-centric real estate solutions. What started as a boutique firm in Sarasota has evolved into a nationally recognized leader, setting new standards for luxury and precision in our industry.

Today, Bicrea is synonymous with precision, luxury, and trust—values that guide every project we undertake. Our reputation has been built through decades of successful partnerships, innovative strategies, and an unwavering dedication to our clients' prosperity.
