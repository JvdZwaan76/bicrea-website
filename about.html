Our Story

Founded in 1990, BicRea has become synonymous with excellence in luxury property investments and mineral rights services, serving distinguished clients across the United States from our headquarters in Sarasota, Florida.

A Legacy Built on Excellence

BicRea began as a vision to transform the luxury real estate investment landscape. What started as a boutique firm serving select clients in Florida has evolved into a nationally recognized leader in property investments and mineral title services, managing over $2.1 billion in assets.

Our journey has been marked by unwavering commitment to our clients' success, meticulous attention to detail, and an ability to identify opportunities where others see only challenges. Through economic cycles and market changes, we have consistently delivered exceptional results while building lasting relationships based on trust and performance.

Today, BicRea stands as a testament to what can be achieved when expertise meets passion, and when a commitment to excellence drives every decision we make.

Our headquarters at 4360 Winners Circle in Sarasota, Florida, positions us at the center of one of the nation's most dynamic luxury real estate markets. Sarasota's combination of cultural sophistication, economic vitality, and natural beauty makes it an ideal base for our operations.
