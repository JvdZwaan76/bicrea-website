How We Work

A clear process, on your timeline

Two specialties, two slightly different paths — but the same operating principles: in writing, in plain language, on a schedule we commit to. Here is what working with BICREA actually looks like.

Mineral Title Engagements

From inquiry to delivered report

Standard mineral title reports follow this six-step path. Complex multi-tract or curative projects extend the research and delivery phases but follow the same structure.

Initial Inquiry

You send tract description, county, scope, and any timing constraints. We respond within one business day with clarifying questions and any additional information we need to scope the work.

Scoped Engagement Letter

Within 24–48 hours of clarifying questions, you receive a written engagement letter — flat fee, defined scope, committed delivery date, and any assumptions or carve-outs. No work begins until you accept and any required retainer is paid.

Research

We pull records from the relevant county clerk's office and any state-level repositories. We build the chain, pull source images, and identify exceptions, encumbrances, and open issues. You get a midpoint status update — not radio silence until delivery.

Internal Review

Every report goes through internal review before it leaves the firm. Conclusions are checked against the source documents, citations are verified, and risk-rated commentary is reviewed for clarity and defensibility.

Delivery and Walk-Through

Final report delivered as a structured PDF with source-documentation index. We schedule a 30-minute call to walk through the findings, answer questions, and discuss any flagged issues that warrant curative or further legal review.

Follow-Up Support

For 30 days after delivery, we answer follow-up questions on the file at no additional charge. Curative work, supplemental research, or report updates beyond the original scope are quoted as new engagements.

Distressed Property Purchases

From first call to clean closing

Distressed property transactions take longer than a regular sale because the underlying issues take real work to resolve. Here is the path, with realistic timing.

Tell Us About the Property

Call, email, or fill out the form. Share what you know and what is making the situation complicated. Specific details help — open code cases, recorded liens, structural issues, anything you are aware of. If you are not sure, that's fine too.

Free Walkthrough

We schedule a property visit at your convenience, typically within a few days. We bring no contracts, no scripts, no closing pressure. Just a real look at the property, conducted respectfully whether you are present or not.

Typical Written Offer Within 72 Hours

You receive a specific written offer with the price, the closing timeline, and a clear list of what we are assuming as part of the transaction. Plain English. One page where possible. The offer is good for a reasonable period — typically 10 days — so you have time to think.

You Decide, On Your Time

Take the offer to an attorney, a real estate agent, a family member, anyone you trust. Get a second opinion. Compare against any other offers you have received. We never push for a same-day signature, and we expect you to consult independent counsel before signing anything.

Contract and Title Work

If you accept, we sign a Florida-compliant purchase agreement. The transaction goes to a reputable Florida title company for escrow and closing. Where Florida law provides a cancellation right, it is honored fully and stated clearly in the contract.

Closing and Funding

Cash funded by us — no financing contingency. Most uncomplicated cash closings typically happen 14 to 30 days from contract. Probate, quiet title, or active code enforcement cases extend the timeline because the underlying legal process drives the schedule. We give you a realistic estimate, not a guess.

Have a project in mind?

Tell us what you are working on and we will tell you what working with us would look like — specifically, in writing, with a real timeline.
