Florida Phosphate Rights

Severed mineral estate research, Polk County, FL

A landowner contacted BICREA to research the status of severed phosphate rights affecting an inherited Florida property. We traced the chain of mineral conveyances back through 80 years of recordation, identified the current owners of the severed interests, and produced a report supporting the landowner's surface-use planning.

Curative Title Work

Missing-heir curative for an Oklahoma royalty interest

A royalty owner had inherited an interest with a broken chain of title — three deceased predecessors and an unrecorded estate distribution. BICREA researched the heirship, located the missing parties, and coordinated with the client's attorney for affidavits of heirship that cured the title for production payment purposes.

Florida property acquisitions

Code Enforcement Resolution

Sarasota County property with $87,000 in code liens

An out-of-state owner inherited a Sarasota County property with five years of accumulated code enforcement liens totaling approximately $87,000 against an estimated $145,000 retail value after repair. We negotiated a lien settlement with the county, purchased the property at a price that left the seller with $42,000 net at closing, and completed the curative and rehabilitation work over the following six months.

Probate Property

Inherited Hillsborough County property with deferred maintenance

Three out-of-state heirs inherited a Tampa-area property requiring an estimated $60,000 in deferred maintenance. None wanted to coordinate repairs or list the home traditionally. BICREA worked with the family's probate counsel through letters testamentary, purchased the property as-is, and closed within 18 days of the court's authorization.

Vacant + Title Issue

Vacant Manatee County property with clouded title

An owner held a property that had sat vacant for three years. Title was clouded by an unreleased mortgage from a prior owner — long-paid but never satisfied of record. We coordinated a curative effort with title counsel, purchased the property contingent on lien release, and closed clean. The owner walked away from a property they had been unable to sell or insure.
